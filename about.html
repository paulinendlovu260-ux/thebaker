About Us

Our aim is to be the community’s favourite Bakery

We are dedicated to bringing you the very best breads and pastries made daily, from scratch and with top quality ingredients. We opened our bakery in Blackrock July 2017, after Pauline, owner and director, realised that there was a demand for more authentic, artisan baked goods. Inspiration taken from bakeries see across Europe through the years and combined recipes from French, Italy and Central Europe with beloved Irish classics, for an eclectic offer that also includes barista coffee and exceptional service.
