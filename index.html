Welcome to Pauline's Bakery

At Pauline’s Bakery we make a diverse range of traditional and contemporary cakes, popular organic sour dough and treats including traditional flaky coffee slices filled with fresh Irish cream, shortbread apple tarts and some special personalised occasion cakes.

The bakery’s range of six sourdough breads are being produced “the long-fermented way – the hard way”.

“A basket full of warmth, straight from our oven to your table,“Golden, crusty, and ready to share.”
